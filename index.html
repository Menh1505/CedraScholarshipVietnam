Cedra Blockchain

Move Platform for Next-Gen dApps

Cedra is a blockchain built on the Move language, focused on high performance, parallel execution, and fast finality - creating a platform for developers to build dApps and make the Move ecosystem more accessible.

Move-first Architecture

Cedra leverages Move's resource model to make smart contracts clearer, safer, and more suitable for real-world Web3 applications.

Performance & Fast Finality

Designed for high throughput with parallel execution and BFT consensus, delivering a smooth user experience.

Public-good & Community

Cedra positions itself as a "public good" for the Move ecosystem, encouraging open collaboration, documentation, and product contributions.
